Kentucky Mule

My favorite cocktail. Simple, tasty, and perfect in warm or cold weather.

Ingredients

  • crushed ice
  • 2oz bourbon
  • 4-6oz of ginger beer
  • 1oz fresh lime juice

Directions

  1. Fill glass with good amount of crushed ice.
  2. Add bourbon.
  3. Add ginger beer.
  4. Add lime juice.
  5. Stir.
  6. Garnish with lime wedge for great insta karma.

Enjoy!
