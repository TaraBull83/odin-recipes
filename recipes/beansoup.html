Bean Soup

Classic senate navy bean soup recipe. Delicious when served alone or with cornbread or a biscuit.

Ingredients

  • 1lb navy beans
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • smoked ham hock or pork jowl (or similar smoked pork product)
  • 8 cups of water or chicken or vegetable stock

Directions

  1. Add beans, carrots, onion, and celery to instant pot.
  2. Add 8 cups of water or stock.
  3. Add ham hock or smoked pork jowl
  4. Pressure cook on high for 30 minutes.
  5. Quick release once cooked. Carefully open after pressure is released.
  6. Salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with parsley, cilantro, or green onion.

Enjoy!
